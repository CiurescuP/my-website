About me

I liked coding in 2021 when I began a 6 months Web Development course on Coursera. Coding became my passion in 2022 when I finished the course and had to learn more about Development and code projects from scratch. Computers have always been in my life, so everything about coding makes sense to me; Designing, the creativity that comes with it, the freedom and debugging.

The internet has come a long way and still, it is constantly changing. That's not a problem for as I am extremely passionate about technology and I enjoy learning new things everyday. When it comes to websites, I am a perfectionist, I am organized, determinated, responsible and I find creative ways to solve a problem.

I'm thrilled to start my Web Developer career, to be a part of the IT world, to share my passions with my colleagues and with the world. My devotion as a Web Developer is to make sure that everyone on all types of devices can easily access a web page. Outside of work, I have a passion for video-games and movies.
